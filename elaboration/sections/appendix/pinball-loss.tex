\section{Pinball loss}
\label{sec:pinball-loss}

The pinball loss \(L\) is defined as: 
\[ L(q_p, y) = \begin{cases}
    (1-p)(q_p - y), &\text{if } y < q_p, \\
    p(y - q_p), &\text{if } y \geq q_p,
\end{cases} \]
where \(p \in \set{0.01, \ldots, 0.99}\) and \(y\) is the observed target.
